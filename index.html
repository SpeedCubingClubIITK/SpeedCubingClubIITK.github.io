About us

Speedcubing Club of IIT Kanpur is a student-run club dedicated to promoting speedcubing. The club aims to provide opportunities for students to improve their speedsolving skills, compete with fellow cubers, and participate in national and international competitions. The club also organizes workshops, projects, training sessions, and events to bring together the speedcubing community of IIT Kanpur.
